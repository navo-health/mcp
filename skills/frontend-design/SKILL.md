# Frontend Design Skill

This skill enables creation of distinctive, production-grade frontend interfaces that prioritize design quality and avoid generic aesthetics.

## Core Design Philosophy

- **Identify purpose and audience** to guide aesthetic decisions
- **Choose a bold directional tone** from options like minimalist, maximalist, retro-futuristic, luxury, brutalist, or art deco approaches
- **Respect technical constraints** while maintaining creative vision
- **Execute with intentionality** rather than defaulting to predictable patterns

The central principle: "Bold maximalism and refined minimalism both work - the key is intentionality, not intensity."

## Implementation Standards

- **Typography**: Distinctive, characterful fonts paired strategically
- **Color & Theme**: Cohesive palettes with dominant colors and sharp accents via CSS variables
- **Motion**: High-impact animations, staggered page-load reveals and scroll-triggered effects
- **Spatial Design**: Asymmetrical layouts with overlap, diagonal flow, and intentional negative space
- **Visual Depth**: Atmospheric backgrounds using gradients, textures, patterns, and creative effects

## Critical Constraints

Avoid generic "AI slop" aesthetics: overused fonts, purple gradient cliches, predictable layouts, and cookie-cutter patterns. Each design should feel genuinely contextualized.
